About EPOCH Time
What is EPOCH Time?

Unix time (also known as EPOCH time) is the number of seconds that have elapsed since January 1, 1970 (midnight UTC/GMT), not counting leap seconds.

Supported Formats:
  • Seconds: 10-digit number (e.g., 1609459200)
  • Milliseconds: 13-digit number (e.g., 1609459200000)
  • DateTime: YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS and various formats
